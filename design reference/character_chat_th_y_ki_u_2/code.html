Chapter I: The Encounter

"Greetings, traveler."

You wish to hear of my journey from the Gia biến to the shores of the Tiền Đường river? The path is etched in sorrow, yet illuminated by fleeting moments of profound beauty.

Tell me about the conflict between your talent and your fate.

Read

Ah, the "Chữ tài chữ mệnh"... it is as if the heavens are jealous of those with too much beauty or skill.

They say talent and destiny are forever at war. I was gifted with poetry, music, and painting, yet these very gifts drew the tempest that shattered my family. Does a flower invite the storm by blooming too brightly?
